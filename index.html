What is a Derivative?

In calculus we talk about lots of new math topics, one being derivatives. But what even is a derivative?

Khan Academy says, The derivative of a function describes the function's instantaneous rate of change at a certain point.

Basically, a derivative is just the slope, or how fast something is changing, at one exact point.

Finding derivatives in calculus can be difficult, but with help from rules like the power rule, product rule, quotient rule, and chain rule, everything becomes a lot easier.
